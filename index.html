From first call to verdict

How a McCann case actually moves.

Five steps. Same five steps for every case we take, whether it's a fender-bender claim or a fatal scaffold collapse. The reason carriers know who we are is because they know how we work.

Step 1

Intake

You call. A real attorney returns the call within the business day. We ask for the police report, the EMT run sheet, and the names of every witness on scene.

Step 2

Reconstruction

Independent crash reconstruction, on-site photography, scene measurement. We do this before the carrier ever opens its file.

Step 3

Demand

We file a documented demand with the carrier. It includes medical specials, future treatment forecasts, and the case theory.

Step 4

Negotiation

The carrier comes back. If the offer matches the case, you decide whether to take it. If it doesn't, we file.

Step 5

Trial

The case goes to a Philadelphia or county jury. Our partners have closed in front of every Common Pleas judge in the city.
